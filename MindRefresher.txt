Rana ne ojha ko laya bataya, 
ki Bhoot ya pret hai Meera ke bheetar?

Girdhar, Girdhar ki rat le,
taj ghoonghat ghoomti hai dar bedar.

Jeebh pe girdhar naam na aa sake, 
mantra koi aisa maariye muh par.

Ojha ne meera ki aankhon mein jhaanka, 
toh ojha bhi gaa utha Girdhar, Girdhar.

- Mamta Sharma
